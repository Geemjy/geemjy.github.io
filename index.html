Biography

Hello, I am Jooyeon Geem (김주연), a postdoctoral researcher at the Asteroid Engineering Laboratory, Space Systems, Luleå University of Technology, Kiruna, Sweden. My supervisor is Dr. Mikael Granvik.
I am interested in the small bodies in the solar system, especially hydrated asteroids. My main research techniques are Polarimetry and Spectroscopy. I am also developing a polarimeter (SQUIDPOL) for solar system small bodies.

* Note: Jooyeon KIM is my passport name. I use GEEM for academic activity due to the overwhelming number of people with the last name KIM.
